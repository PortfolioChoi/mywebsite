MY DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

**Design is not merely about creating forms.** Kenya Hara once said that design is "the process of eliminating the unnecessary and leaving only the essential." Likewise, in my design process, I strive to remove unnecessary complexity and ensure that the core message is conveyed with clarity. Moreover, I do not focus solely on form; I deeply explore the meaning and emotional impact of design, aiming to create experiences that users can intuitively understand and engage with. Ultimately, I believe that design is not a fixed outcome but a continuously evolving and expanding experience. As time, culture, and technology change, design is constantly reinterpreted and reborn. Therefore, I perceive design not as a static result but as an ever-evolving flow, where flexible thinking enables natural interactions between design and its users. My goal is to create designs that seamlessly integrate into people’s lives, fostering meaningful interactions. I aspire to be a designer who sees the world with fresh perspectives and creates designs that hold deeper meaning. Thank you.
